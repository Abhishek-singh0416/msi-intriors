Our Company

Dream it, design it, live it. We infuse your essence into artistic masterpieces

MSI Interiors is a leading concept interiors and decor company in the luxury segment, based in Delhi NCR. Since 2018, we have been transforming spaces into artistic masterpieces that reflect the unique personality and style of our clients.

Our carefully crafted bouquet of interior solutions spans everything from architectural design, facade and elevation design, interior design, to custom furniture, accessories, artworks, and landscaping. Our comprehensive end-to-end, turn-key solutions are aimed at all interiors from apartments and penthouses to bungalows, villas and farmhouses.

As one of the best Interior designing firm in Gurgaon & NCR, we take up projects pan-India and have built an impressive portfolio that speaks to our commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction.

At MSI Interiors, we believe interiors are not just rooms, they are living poems waiting to be penned. Our passion is creating spaces that nourish your soul and inspire your spirit, wholeheartedly designed around your own lifestyle and demands.

From carefully designed furniture layouts and colour palettes to rigorous attention to every detail, we guarantee a smooth and stress-free experience to create something that you have always envisioned!

Whether you desire the clean lines of modern minimalism, the timeless allure of heirloom pieces, or the whimsical touch of personalized details, we curate designs for all your needs. From statement sofas that enfold conversation to hidden storage solutions that optimize space, we create pieces that not only complement your existing décor but uplift your entire living experience.

Meet Our Founder

Visionary Leader Behind MSI Interiors

RAJA BABU

Director & Principal Designer

Raja Babu, the founder and principal designer of MSI Interiors, has been recognized as one of Delhi NCR's leading Interior Designers. He has been valued incessantly for his authentic designs made with uncompromised craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Since the establishment of MSI Interiors in 2018, Raja Babu has focused on the pivotal strengths of innovation, an eye for intricate detailing, and understanding the client's orientation for their space. His dedication to delivering spaces that are artistically refined with global standards and completing work within confined timelines has made him a noteworthy name in the domain of interior design.

"Accessories and enhancements are the elements that bring out the personality of a space and give the final touch of balance and style. Every project is an opportunity to create something extraordinary that resonates with the client's vision."

With formal education in interior design and a deep appreciation for lifestyle aesthetics, Raja Babu strives to provide the best solutions tailored to each client's needs. In a field rife with change, his exposure to global design trends has made him a competent designer who can constantly deliver contemporary excellence.

Raja Babu also has expertise in product design that is in complete harmony with signature design intents. From fabrics to home decor, enhancements to furniture, his designs are elegant, budget-friendly, and majestic. The experimental design approach is taking elements of design to a new realm of luxury living while maintaining affordability.

What sets him apart is not just the talent for creating beautiful spaces, but also the unwavering dedication to understanding the unique needs and preferences of each client. His accomplishments can be attributed to his talent, determination, and pure ardor for design.
